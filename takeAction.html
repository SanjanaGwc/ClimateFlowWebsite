Lifestyle:
In order to adopt a healthier & cleaner lifestyle for both you and the planet, consider becoming a vegetarian. Reducing our consumption of meat can lead to less methane produced as the livestock reared is decreased. Recycling and repurposing used products can have a significant influence on the world. In order to dispose of items in a safe way, local recycling centers are the best. Click on the recycling tab on the left to find recycling centers near you.

Easy Changes
Implementing lifestyle changes to reduce the stress inflicted upon the planet, does not need to be cumbersome. Easy changes that can help decrease the amount of non-degradable items in a household is through repurposing. Old, unused clothes can be donated to thrift stores.


Products
Non-biodegradable products sold in department stores can be hazardous for the environment, however can be hardly be avoided. However, there are eco-friendly substitutes that retain quality and are low cost. Plenty of brands have made it their mission to protect the environment through their products. They have achieved this goal by creating packages that are 100% recyclable.


Other Methods
Further intervention can be achieved by supporting local businesses that have taken up a eco-friendly intervention. A larger impact can be made by supporting local representatives whose platforms focus on supporting laws and campaigns dealing with environmental issues.
